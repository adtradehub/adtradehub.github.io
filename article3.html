How to Use Monetag to Earn Daily (2026 Guide)

Monetag is one of the easiest ways to earn daily income from your website, even if your traffic is small. You can earn through popunder ads, push notifications, direct links, and in-page push ads.

1. Popunder Ads (Highest Earnings)

Popunder ads generate the highest revenue for most users. You just paste the code inside your website, and Monetag handles everything automatically.

  • High CPM
  • Works with all traffic
  • Best for beginners

2. In-Page Push Ads

These show as small notifications inside your website. They perform well on mobile.

3. Push Notification Ads

Visitors subscribe to notifications, and you earn money whenever they click ads later.

4. Monetag Direct Link (SmartLink)

A simple link that earns money automatically. Perfect for social media users.

5. Tips to Increase Daily Earnings

  • Use all Monetag formats together
  • Publish daily content
  • Promote your site on Pinterest
  • Target mobile users
  • Use smart placements for more clicks

Conclusion

Monetag is a powerful earning platform for beginners. With the right setup and traffic sources, you can earn daily even with low traffic.
