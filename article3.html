How to Use Monetag to Earn Daily (Beginner Guide 2026)

Monetag is one of the easiest platforms for beginners to earn daily money through ads—without needing a website with heavy traffic. In this guide, you'll learn how Monetag works, the best ad formats, and how to maximize your earnings.

What Is Monetag?

Monetag is an ad network that helps you monetize websites, landing pages, or even simple HTML pages. They accept beginners, allow low-traffic sites, and offer high-earning ad formats such as Popunder, In-Page Push, SmartLink, and more.

Best Monetag Ad Formats

1. In-Page Push

These look like notifications inside the page. High CTR and works well on mobile.

2. Popunder Ads

Opens behind the main browser window. Highest earnings among all formats.

3. SmartLink

A single link that automatically sends users to the highest-paying offer based on their country and device.

4. Floating Banner

A small banner that sticks to the bottom of the screen. Minimal disturbance + consistent earnings.

How to Set Up Monetag Ads

  1. Create an account at Monetag.com
  2. Add your website or landing page
  3. Verify ownership (meta tag or HTML file)
  4. Choose ad formats and generate the code
  5. Paste the code into your website

Tips to Increase Monetag Earnings

  • Use 2–3 ad formats together (In-Page Push + Popunder + Floating Banner)
  • Promote your website on Pinterest or Quora
  • Use SmartLink on WhatsApp, Telegram, YouTube descriptions
  • Update your site regularly to maintain traffic

Conclusion

Monetag is a great earning option for beginners who want fast daily earnings with simple setup. Use multiple ad formats and drive traffic consistently for the best results.
