How to Use Monetag to Earn Daily (Beginner-Friendly Guide)

Monetag is one of the easiest ad networks for beginners to start earning money online. Whether you have a website, a landing page, or even low traffic, Monetag can help you earn daily with multiple ad formats.

Why Monetag Works for Beginners

  • Easy approval
  • Ads work even with low traffic
  • Multiple earning formats
  • Fast payouts
  • Works on mobile websites

Best Monetag Ad Formats for Earnings

1. In-Page Push

High CTR and great for mobile users. Shows a small push-style notification on the page.

2. Popunder Ads

Opens a promotional page in a background tab. High earnings but use carefully.

3. Floating Banner

A clean banner at the bottom of your website. Beginner-friendly, no disturbance.

4. SmartLink

You can promote this link anywhere: social media, WhatsApp, Pinterest, or YouTube.

How to Increase Monetag Earnings

  • Add In-Page Push + Floating Banner together
  • Write more blog content → more pageviews → more earnings
  • Promote SmartLink for extra traffic
  • Use Pinterest pins to drive visitors

Conclusion

Monetag is perfect for beginners who want daily earnings with zero investment. Add the ads correctly, increase your traffic, and earnings will grow over time.
