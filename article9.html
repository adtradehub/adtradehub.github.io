Best Keyword Research Tips (2026 Guide)

Best Tools for Keyword Research →

Finding the right keywords is the foundation of SEO. If you target the right phrases, you can rank faster and get more traffic even with a new blog.

1. Choose Low-Competition Keywords

Beginners should focus on keywords with:

  • Low difficulty
  • Medium search volume
  • Clear user intent

2. Use Free Keyword Tools

You can find great keywords using tools like:

  • Keyword Surfer
  • Ubersuggest (free version)
  • Google Trends
  • Ahrefs Free Tools
Explore Advanced SEO Tools →

3. Use Long-Tail Keywords

Examples:

  • “how to start affiliate marketing for free”
  • “best Pinterest SEO tips 2026”
  • “simple digital products to sell online”

4. Study Search Intent

Understand what users actually want:

  • Informational (learn something)
  • Transactional (buy something)
  • Navigational (find a site)

5. Analyze Competitors

Search your keyword and check:

  • Top 10 results
  • Word count
  • Headings
  • Quality

6. Look for Keyword Variations

  • People Also Ask
  • Related Searches
  • Autocomplete Keywords

7. Create Content Better Than Competitors

Use:

  • Clear headings
  • Simple language
  • Examples
  • Tools list
  • FAQs

Conclusion

Keyword research becomes easy with the right tools and understanding user intent. Focus on long-tail, low-competition keywords to grow faster.
