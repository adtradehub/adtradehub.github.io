How to Create Pinterest Pins That Get Views & Clicks (2025 Guide)

Pinterest is one of the fastest sources of free traffic. A single viral pin can bring thousands of visitors to your blog or affiliate link — even if your account is new. The key is designing high-quality pins with clear messaging, SEO optimization, and the right posting strategy.

In this guide, you’ll learn step-by-step how to create Pinterest pins that actually get views, saves, and clicks in 2025.

1. Use the Correct Pin Size

Pinterest prefers vertical pins. The best size is:

  • 1000 × 1500 pixels (recommended)
  • 1080 × 1920 pixels (story pins)

These sizes display perfectly in the home feed and search feed.

2. Use High-Contrast Colors

Pins with strong, clear colors stand out more. Use:

  • Dark text on light background
  • Light text on dark background
  • Brand colors for consistency

Colors that get more clicks:

  • Blue
  • Orange
  • Pink
  • Yellow

3. Add Clear, Bold Text

Your pin should answer the viewer’s question instantly. Use short, bold text like:

  • “Make Money Online in 2025”
  • “Best Side Hustles for Beginners”
  • “Affiliate Marketing Guide”
  • “Simple Productivity Tips”

Avoid long sentences. Pinterest is visually fast-paced — clarity wins.

4. Use Pin Templates (Canva Free)

You don’t need to be a designer. Canva offers thousands of free Pinterest templates.

Best templates for your niche:

  • Blog post pins
  • Listicle pins
  • Minimalist designs
  • Bold headline pins

Always customize the colors and text to match your brand.

5. Add Your Logo

Pinterest likes original content. Adding your logo builds brand trust and prevents others from copying your pins.

Place your logo:

  • Bottom center
  • Bottom left
  • Bottom right

6. Write Keyword-Rich Titles

Your title helps Pinterest understand your content.

Examples:

  • “Affiliate Marketing for Beginners (2025)”
  • “Easy Ways to Earn Money Online”
  • “Simple Tutorials to Start Blogging”

7. Write Strong Descriptions

Use keywords naturally inside your description.

Example description:

Learn how to create Pinterest pins that get clicks and views fast. Perfect for beginners in affiliate marketing, blogging, and online income.

8. Add the Correct Link

Your link should go to:

  • A blog article
  • An affiliate landing page
  • A SmartLink page
  • Your homepage

Never leave the URL empty — you’ll lose traffic.

9. Create 3–5 Pins for Each Blog Post

Different designs attract different people. Publishing multiple pin variations increases your chances of going viral.

Examples for one article:

  • Pin with bold text
  • Minimal pin
  • Photo-based pin
  • Text-only pin

10. Use Pinterest SEO

Pinterest is a visual search engine. Use keywords in:

  • Title
  • Description
  • Board name
  • Your pin text
  • Profile bio

Bonus: Best Niches for Pinterest in 2025

  • Make money online
  • Affiliate marketing
  • Budget planning
  • Cooking/recipes
  • DIY crafts
  • Home decor
  • Fashion
  • Fitness & health

Conclusion

Creating Pinterest pins that get traffic is not difficult. With the right design, strong headlines, and proper SEO, your pins can reach thousands of users — even if your account is new. Keep publishing consistently, use beautiful colors, and focus on keyword-rich content. Pinterest rewards effort and consistency.
